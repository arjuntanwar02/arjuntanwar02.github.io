About Tanwar Thesis

A public investment journal documenting equity research, macro regime analysis, and disciplined capital allocation frameworks.

Purpose

Tanwar Thesis exists to document investment thinking in real time. Every thesis published is transparent, data-driven, and accountable. The objective is to build a structured archive of decision-making â€” not to provide predictions, but to refine process.

Investment Philosophy

The framework prioritizes long-duration growth, strong competitive positioning, balance sheet strength, and asymmetric risk-reward opportunities. Macro conditions are incorporated when they materially impact valuation or capital allocation decisions.

Research Structure

  • Clear investment question
  • Revenue and margin analysis
  • Competitive landscape assessment
  • Valuation modeling and sensitivity
  • Defined risk factors
